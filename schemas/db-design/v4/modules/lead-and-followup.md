# 客户与跟进

集合：
- `hs_lead_customer`
- `hs_lead_requirement`
- `hs_lead_appointment`
- `hs_lead_followup`
- `hs_lead_match`

字段要点：
- 客户主档：`phone` `owner_staff_id` `customer_tags`
- 需求快照：`budget` `area` `rent_mode` `source_type`
- 预约看房：`appointment_time` `appointment_status` `deal_result`
- 跟进记录：`followup_type` `content` `next_action`
- 匹配记录：`listing_ids` `match_reason`
